Pestilent Tides

How To Play

Select an enemy (Currently only zombie and slime are fully functional, only zombie is animated).

Select attacks to defeat your enemies. Specials and Healing have a cooldown, so use them wisely.

All attacks have a chance to hit. The more damage they deal, the more they will miss. Plan out your attacks accordingly.
